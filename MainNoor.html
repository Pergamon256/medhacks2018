InforMED

Many high impact research articles are hidden behind paywalls and largely inaccessible to academics and patients alike. What InforMED does is it leverages the academic community and crowdsourcing in order to provide accurate summaries that are useful to both other academics across fields as well as patients and people interested in specific topics. It also provides a community-based discussion platform that allows users and contributors to upvote good discussion and connect the academics with the interested readers. As people in journal clubs already do this, however in a manner that does not save any information, members of these journal clubs could also conrtibute to InforMED and provide there article and research summaries here as well.
